The Prompt

Calling all aspiring writers and creative thinkers!
Henry, IL needs a superhero, and we're counting on you to create one.

In an essay of 500-1000 words, describe your original Marvel-style superhero, who originates from Henry.
Be sure to include the following:

  • The name of your superhero and their backstory. How did they gain their power? What motivated them to become a crime-fighter?
  • The kinds of crime your superhero fights. What kinds of villains or threats are they up against? How does your superhero's power help them combat these crimes?
  • How does your superhero reflect the spirit and values of Henry, IL? What makes them unique and different from other superheroes?
We're looking for essays that are both creative and entertaining, so feel free to let your imagination run wild! The winner of the scholarship will receive $1500 towards their education, so put your best foot forward and show us what you've got.

About the scholarship

The 2023 winner will be announced in-person at the HSHS Scholarship Awards Presentation

2023 is the second annual essay contest for graduating Henry Senachewine High School seniors going onto higher education. This $1500 scholarship will be awarded to the student that can demonstrate they have their ducks in a row.

Qualifications

  • Applicants must be a graduating senior at Henry Senachwine High School.
  • Students must be continuing onto further higher education. University, community college, or trade schools are eligible.
  • One entry per student. Multiple entries will be disregarded.
  • Essays must be submitted online by April 29th, 2023. No later than 11:59 pm Mountain Daylight Time (US)
  • There is no age or citizenship requirement.
  • The Winner must show a valid student ID or proof of enrollment at Henry Senachwine High School.
  • All entries will become property of The Educated Duck. Winner's essay will be used as examples for future Educated Duck Scholarships.
  • The decision of The Educated Duck is final.

How To Apply

Applications for the scholarship contest can be submitted by clicking the Apply for Scholarship button.
